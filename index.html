CONNECT FOUR

  • Be the first player to get four of your colored checkers in a row - horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • Players will alternate turns after playing a checker.
  • On your turn, drop one of your checkers down any of the slots at the top of the grid.
  • The first to get four checkers of their color in a row wins!
  • Press ESC to pause the game.

    • Good Luck!
